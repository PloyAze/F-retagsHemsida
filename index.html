Capsule Corporation (カプセルコーポレーション Kapuseru Kōporēshon) is a company

founded in Age 712,[2] and is run by Dr. Brief, Bulma's father. The corporation

is based around, and takes its name from, the Hoi-Poi Capsules (also known as DynoCaps)

invented by Dr. Brief himself.

Due to the invention and production of the extremely popular and useful capsules,

the Capsule Corporation is one of the largest, richest and most influential

companies in the world. At one point, the Capsule Corporation and the Red Ribbon

Army were competitive rivals in the business race, but the creation of the DynoCaps

from Capsule Corporation resulted in the Red Ribbon Army losing to it.

DynoCaps

Capsules, also known as DynoCaps or Hoi-Poi Capsules, were created by Dr. Brief of the Capsule Corporation, for the purpose of making objects compact and easy to transport.

Radar

Dragon Radar, Bleeper in the Ocean Group dubs, and Dragon Ball Energy Locator in the live-action film, Dragon Ball Evolution, is a tracking device that makes finding the Dragon Balls more efficient, compared to conventional methods.

Spacecrafts

Spacecrafts, pod, this sphere with legs is something that penetrates through the earths atmosphere and enters space. It uses highly compact fuel that realise power to make the craft move at a faster speed than regular rockets.

Time Machine

The Time Machine is a machine built by Bulma from the future in Future Trunks' alternate timeline, that could be used to go to the future or to the past. The word "Hope!!" is written on the side of the machine, emphasizing that it was hope for their world.
